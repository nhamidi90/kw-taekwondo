Children's Classes

TUESDAY

4:00-5:15pm

Children aged 4-12 years are welcome to join. It is suitable for all levels and comprises of group exercises. It gives the ability for your child to develop thier skills and confidence at their own pace.

For those aged 8 and above, there will be a special focus on the ITF (International Taekwondo Federation) syllabus. We will prepare them should they wish to go throught the grading system.

Prices per session: 4-7 years: £4
8-12 years: £5

Ladies Classes

WEDNESDAY

7:30-9:00pm

This class is for women only and provides a safe space for learning without all the pressure holding you back from taking that first step to a new you. You don't need to have any prior knowledge or ability as you will work from the ground up. The classes aim to work you through the ITF syllabus from white to black belt and students will be working closely with their seniors to gain a range of experience. There will be lots of exercises and focus on patterns and techniques.

Price: £20 a month
